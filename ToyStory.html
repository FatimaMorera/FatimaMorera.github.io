Review for Toy Story

Director: John Lasseter

This movie is about a kid named Andy who loves his toys. When Buzz arrived, his new toy, he took Woody's place. Woody used to be Andy's favorite doll and the one he took everywhere. Woody was jealous of Buzz and he tried to get rid of him. Andy had no idea that his toys were not normal and that when he was not there they started talking and walking. at the end, Woody and Buzz got lost and they had to find a way to get back home before Andy and his family moved without them to another home.
